💡 TIPS & SHORTCUTS

🃏 Add a Card to Inventory first, then log a Sale linked to it for full P&L tracking.

🎪 Shows automatically feed into the master Dashboard and Analytics.

📊 Platform fees are deducted from profit automatically in the Sales Log.

👀 Watchlist helps you track cards you want to buy before they appear in your inventory.

💸 Expenses (packaging, tables, travel) are tracked separately and shown in True Net Profit.

⬇ Export CSV regularly to back up your data — it lives in your browser only.
